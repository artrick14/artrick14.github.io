It's Been a Purrrrrfect September

September 26, 2022

Hello All,

Kiki Palmer here. This September has been action packed! Mom and dad have been letting me explore some more. They still don’t trust me to go outside unleashed because I am such an adrenaline junky and will hop any fence if I see something cool outside. One night, our neighbor’s cat was loose—I saw it and launched myself right over the fence!

I have had lots of visitors come see me this month. The most notable one was my cousin Parmesan, she’s a golden retriever. She hasn’t grasp the fact that I don’t speak dog, so I didn’t understand A WORD she was saying when she was trying to talk to me. I did really well though. It was so nice seeing my family members.

I also have been busting it down at night

My goals for October are to see a bat, do some more yoga and see the musical Cats. I also want to see my fellow feline friends The Panthers play but I don’t understand much football, so it may be a waste of a ticket if mom and dad get me one. Have been craving a glizzy though. I also want to ride a roller coaster, but Dad said I will never meet the height and weight requirements for Fury 365 at Carowinds. I have also been making hella biscuits.

TTYL XOX,

Kiki Palmer
